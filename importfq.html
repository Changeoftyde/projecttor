Instructions:
We are sorry we can't show you how to import due to some copyright issues...but we can tell you!
Make sure you follow these instructions carefully to ensure your file gets imported correctly. :)

- Step 1: Go to the Quizlet website, not the app.
- Step 2: Open one of your Quizlet sets. If you want to import someone else's, make a copy and then open the copy in your library.
- Step 3: Find the icon (it should be in the top-right corner) that displays three dots (•••).
- Step 4: Click "Export."
- Step 5: Under "Between term and definition," click "Custom" and enter \\\ in the blank. Make sure you use backslashes (\\\) and not slashes (///).
- Step 6: Under "Between rows," click "Custom" and enter \\\ in the blank. Make sure you use backslashes (\\\) and not slashes (///).
- Step 7: Click "Copy Text."
- Step 8: Paste your text in the blank below.
